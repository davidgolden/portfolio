About

I am a front-end web developer. My front-end stack includes HTML5, CSS3 (vanilla and Sass), and vanilla Javascript. I have had some exposure to Bootstrap, jQuery, and server-side languages PHP and NodeJS. I am self-taught, having completed the front-end component of Colt Steele's Web Development Bootcamp, worked through Lynda courses, and studied StackExchange as well as many other other online resources.

Outside of web development, I hold a B.S. in Environmental Science from Dickinson College. I own a curbside composting company in Durango, CO. And my most time-consuming hobby is ultra distance trail running in the mountains.
